Cricket History

First International Match (1877)

The first Test match was played between England and Australia in 1877 at the MCG.

First Cricket World Cup (1975)

The inaugural Cricket World Cup was hosted by England in 1975, and West Indies won.

Cricket Records

Most Runs in International Cricket

Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for most international runs, with over 34,000 runs.

Most Wickets in Tests

Muttiah Muralitharan has the highest wickets in Test cricket with 800 wickets.

Legendary Players

Sir Don Bradman

Bradman, with an average of 99.94 in Test cricket, is considered the greatest batsman ever.

Virat Kohli

One of the modern greats, Kohli has redefined consistency in all formats.
